Instructions
Read 10 chapters per day — one from each of 10 lists organized by biblical genre: Gospels, Law, NT Letters (1 & 2), Wisdom, Psalms, Proverbs, OT History, Prophets, and Acts. Because each list has a different number of chapters, they cycle independently — the combination of readings changes every day and never repeats. Use "Next" to advance through your reading lists — your progress is saved automatically. Select any text to highlight it in one of several colors, and use "Highlights" to browse and revisit everything you've marked.
