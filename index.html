Instructions

Read 10 chapters per day — one from each of 10 lists organized by biblical genre: Gospels, Law, NT Letters (1 & 2), Wisdom, Psalms, Proverbs, OT History, Prophets, and Acts. Because each list has a different number of books, they cycle independently — the combination of daily readings changes every day and never repeats.

Use "My Progress" to track where you are in each list, as well as where you are within each book.

Use "Next" to advance through your reading lists — your progress is saved automatically.

Select any text to highlight it in one of several colors, and use "Highlights" to browse and revisit everything you've marked.

Use "Search" to search through the entire bible.
